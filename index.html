ABOUT

I grew up in Des Moines, IA. At a young age, I began playing the violin and had aspired to be a professional one day. I had encapsulated myself in a creative environment and continued to learn and study about the arts. Unfortunately, I decided the professional musician gig would prove too stressful and frustrating. Later, I moved to Los Angeles and became a Sound Editor. I forced myself to conform to the lifestyle of a dedicated Sound Editor,but the work was inordinately difficult and less creative. My only choice was to job search until I found a job. Web development came later, as I had known absolutely nothing about being a developer of even the basics outside of social media. I started from the very beginning, and went back to school. I'm now a Front End Web Developer and I've never been happier.

As a determined Front End Web developer, I build structure to chaos utilizing passion, creativity, and results-oriented thought patterns to achieve a sound results.
